**The Glonary Awards employ a three-tiered judging model:**

● **Verification:** each submission undergoes an initial review to ensure completeness and compliance with entry requirements.

● **Anonymous expert evaluation:** every entry is anonymized and independently assessed by jury members with relevant industry expertise.

● **Final deliberation:** top-scoring entries are reviewed collectively by the panels to determine finalists and winners.

**Our jury of international experts scores every submission according to the following criteria:**

● **Innovation & Uniqueness:** does the project introduce something new, original, or disruptive to its industry?

● **Execution & Quality:** how well is the product, service, or initiative implemented or delivered? Is it professionally executed?

● **Design & Aesthetics:** is the visual presentation refined and aligned with the brand's identity? (for product/service/branding entries)

● **Impact & Reach:** what measurable results or social/cultural influence has the project achieved? Who benefits from it?

● **Strategic Thinking:** is there a clear vision behind the project? Does it show strategic positioning and market relevance?

● **Public Reception & Reputation:** what kind of feedback has the project received from its audience or community? Is there trust and loyalty?

A comprehensive overview of the judging process is available on the official Glonary Awards website: https://glonaryawards.com/influencersandinfobusiness#judging.
